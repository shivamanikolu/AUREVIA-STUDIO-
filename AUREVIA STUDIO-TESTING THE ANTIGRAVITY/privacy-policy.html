Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 27, 2026

1. Introduction

At Aurevia Studio, accessible from hello@aureviastudio.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Aurevia Studio and how we use it.

2. Information Collection Notice

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out our contact and booking forms, including:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Preferred Appointment Dates
  • Any custom messages detailing clinical requirements

3. Cookies Notice

Like any other website, Aurevia Studio uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

4. Third-Party Analytics

We may support integrations with third-party tracking services (such as Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, and Hotjar) to analyze visitor traffic patterns. These third-party services collect standard logging indicators anonymously, and do not compile any raw medical or prescription detail.

5. Data Retention Disclaimer

We retain your contact details only as long as necessary to process appointment booking requests and maintain basic communication history. We do not sell or lease your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

6. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
